Threat Categories

  • Application: Threats related to software applications developed for mobile devices.
  • Authentication: Threats related to authentication mechanisms.
  • Cellular: Threats related to cellular systems and infrastructure.
  • Ecosystem: Threats related to the greater mobile ecosystem.
  • EMM: Threats related to enterprise mobility management systems.
  • GPS: Threats related to GPS technology.
  • LAN & PAN: Threats related to local and personal area networks.
  • Payment: Threats related to mobile payments.
  • Physical Access: Threats originating from outside of the device.
  • Privacy: Threats related to user privacy.
  • Stack: Threats related to the hardware, firmware, and software used to host and operate a device.
  • Supply Chain: Threats related to the device and component supply chain.
